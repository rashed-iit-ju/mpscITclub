OUR STORY



IT was 1st October of 2018 when we started our journey with a vision to Empower the students of Mohammadpur Preparatory School and College through IT as well as to create unity among them.

With the adjuvant and support of the members of our Club along with the cordiality of our Executives,we conduct a fest within the institution premises in the year 2019. That was our First Fest named "MPSC 1st Inter Class IT Carnival" which took place
on 6th & 7th March of 2019. The winners of the various events of this fest were awarded on 7th March in a splendour way!!

In order to spur the students of different classes to be involved in IT related task,we took various classes on MS Word, MS Powerpoint,Programming and some other basic topics .The studnets had much enthusiasm to learn and they showed
continuous efforts which results in their participation in the "National Child & Teen Programming Contest 2018".

In the same year,during October, we successfully conduct another fest named "MPSC 2nd IT Carnival". We had different interesting events along with "TECHNOBUZZ" in this fest. Due to the friendly behaviour,co-ordination and astute leading of our
Moderators, Mohammad Rasheduzzaman Sir and Farzana Hoque Nishi Miss,

"We Make IT Happen!!!"

With some expectancy and target,we have started the journey of another year along with the New Executive Committee. Hopefully,with all your cooperation and clinch, we will be able to go
ahead and fulfill all our expectancy regarding you!!

So,stay inlaid with us and

"Empower Yourself Through IT"
